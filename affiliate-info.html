Your referral link

When you log in to your dashboard, you'll see a link like this:

https://hyperkey.tn/products.html?ref=sara-ben-ali

When someone clicks your link, the ref parameter is captured and stays with them through checkout — no cookies needed. Your affiliate code is shown at checkout and stored with every order they place.

Rules & guidelines

Referral codes are personal and should not be shared or transferred.
Commission is not paid on cancelled or refunded orders.
Fraudulent or self-referral orders may result in account suspension.
Payouts are processed on the admin's schedule after approval.
Program terms may be updated at any time.

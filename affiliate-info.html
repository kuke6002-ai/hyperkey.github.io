Commission details

How commissions work

Per-category rates. Each product category has its own percentage. A category at 10% earns you 10% of every product price in that category.
Earned on delivery. Commission is calculated when the admin marks the order as delivered. No commission on cancelled orders.
Entire order counts. You earn on every product in a referred order, each at its own category rate.
Dashboard visibility. Your dashboard shows pending commissions and your total paid-out amount separately.

Get started

Become an affiliate

Affiliate accounts are created by the admin — there is no public signup form.

Contact HyperKey Store through the available support channels. Once your account is created, you receive:

Your unique affiliate code
A login password for the affiliate dashboard
Your personal referral link to start sharing
Log in to your dashboard

Your referral link

Your link looks like this:

https://hyperkey.store/products.html?ref=your-code

When a customer clicks your link, the ref parameter is captured automatically and stays with them through checkout — no cookies needed. Your code is shown at checkout and stored with every order.

Rules & guidelines

Referral codes are personal and should not be shared or transferred.
Commission is not paid on cancelled or refunded orders.
Fraudulent or self-referral orders may result in account suspension.
Payouts are processed on the admin's schedule after approval.
Program terms may be updated at any time.

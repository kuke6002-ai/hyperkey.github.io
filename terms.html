Terms, delivery & refund policy

Clear rules for manual payments, WhatsApp delivery, and digital-product support.

These terms apply to orders placed through HyperKey Store. By submitting an order, you confirm that you understand and accept these rules.

1. What HyperKey Store sells

HyperKey Store sells digital products for customers in Tunisia, including game keys, gift cards, game top-ups, subscriptions, software licenses, streaming products, and other digital services.

No physical product is shipped. Delivery is handled digitally after payment review.

2. Manual payment verification

Payments are checked manually. A checkout submission, payment button click, payment reference, transaction ID, authorization number, or recharge card code is not proof of payment by itself.

An order becomes ready for delivery only after HyperKey Store verifies the payment amount and payment details.

For D17 and Flouci, enter only the requested reference or authorization number from your receipt. For TT recharge card payment, enter the required 15 digit card codes exactly as requested on the payment page.

3. Prices, fees, and exact amount

Prices are shown in TND. The amount to verify may change depending on the selected payment method because some methods include manual-payment fees or rounding rules.

Always send the exact amount shown on the payment page. Sending a lower amount, wrong amount, incomplete reference, or wrong payment method can delay or block the order.

4. Order ID and order status

After checkout, the website shows a short Order ID. Copy and save it. You need this Order ID with your WhatsApp number to check the order status and receive delivery details from the order-status page.

If you lose your Order ID, support may still try to help, but lookup and delivery can be delayed.

5. Delivery

Delivery is made through the WhatsApp number provided at checkout and through the order-status page when delivery details are ready.

Some products, especially top-ups, may require extra delivery information after payment is verified, such as a player ID or account ID. If requested, enter that information from the order-status page.

Delivery time is an estimate and may be affected by manual verification, supplier availability, platform problems, wrong customer information, or high order volume.

6. Customer responsibility

Before ordering, check the product name, variation, value, region, platform, and account compatibility. You are responsible for entering the correct WhatsApp number and any requested delivery information.

HyperKey Store is not responsible for problems caused by wrong customer details, wrong product selection, incompatible account region, platform restrictions, or a delivered code/key being shared, used, or redeemed after delivery.

7. Refunds and replacements

Digital products can often be copied, used, transferred, or redeemed immediately. For that reason, refunds are limited.

A refund or replacement may be offered if payment is verified and HyperKey Store cannot fulfil the order, if the product is unavailable after payment, or if HyperKey Store delivers the wrong or invalid product by mistake.

Refunds are normally not available after a code, key, account, subscription, top-up, delivery note, or digital item has been delivered, used, redeemed, activated, shared, or made visible to the customer.

Refunds are also normally not available for wrong product choice, wrong platform, wrong region, customer account restrictions, missing customer delivery information, or delays caused by incorrect payment details.

If there is a real delivery issue, contact support as soon as possible with your Order ID and clear evidence. Do not redeem, share, edit, or resell the delivered item while the issue is being reviewed.

8. Availability and cancellation

Product availability can change. If a product becomes unavailable after payment is verified, HyperKey Store may offer a replacement, store credit, or refund.

HyperKey Store may cancel an order before delivery because of stock problems, suspected fraud, invalid payment information, obvious pricing errors, or technical issues.

9. Safety and private information

Never send passwords, account passwords, bank-card PINs, CVV codes, one-time verification codes, or private account recovery information. HyperKey Store only asks for the information needed to verify payment and deliver the order.

10. Third-party platforms

HyperKey Store is an independent store. It is not officially affiliated with Steam, Xbox, PlayStation, Free Fire, Roblox, Netflix, OpenAI, or any other third-party brand unless clearly stated.

Third-party platforms may have their own rules, region limits, account restrictions, redemption rules, or service interruptions.

11. Privacy

We use customer information only to process, verify, deliver, track, and support orders. Read the Privacy Policy for more details.
